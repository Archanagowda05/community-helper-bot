# Community History

## The Beginning (2020)

Our community was founded in **March 2020** by a small group of 12 developers who wanted to create a supportive learning environment during the global pandemic. What started as a small Discord server quickly grew as people sought connection and collaboration during lockdowns.

## Growth & Milestones

### 2020 — Foundation Year
- Community founded with 12 members
- First Discord server launched
- Established core rules and guidelines
- Reached 200 members by end of year

### 2021 — Building Structure
- Reached **1,000 members**
- Launched the **mentorship program**
- Introduced the role system (Member → Contributor → Moderator)
- Started the weekly newsletter

### 2022 — Going Big
- First **Annual Community Summit** held in September
- Launched the **open-source initiative** with 5 projects
- Community forum went live
- Reached 2,500 members

### 2023 — Recognition & Partnerships
- Surpassed **5,000 members**
- Partnered with tech companies for sponsored events
- Launched career support and job board channels
- Won "Best Online Community" award from TechConnect

### 2024 — Innovation
- Launched the **AI Community Assistant** chatbot
- Expanded globally with regional sub-communities
- Introduced multilingual support
- Started annual scholarship program for members

## Looking Ahead

We continue to grow and evolve, always keeping our core mission at the center: building a supportive, inclusive space for learning and collaboration.
