# TechNexus Events

TechNexus Community organizes a continuous calendar of technology-focused events including meetups, workshops, bootcamps, and conferences. These events are designed for learning, networking, innovation, and collaboration across the tech community.

Events are conducted in Bengaluru, Chennai, Srivilliputhur, partner campuses, and online/hybrid platforms.

## Registration & Entry

Most events require:
- RSVP / Registration  
- Event pass or confirmation  
- Joining the official TechNexus WhatsApp group  

For high-demand or Microsoft-hosted events:
- WhatsApp confirmation is mandatory  
- Double verification may be required  
- Only approved participants with passes are allowed entry  

Participants are advised to:
- Arrive at least 30 minutes early for check-in
