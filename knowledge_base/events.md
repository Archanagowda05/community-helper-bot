# TechNexus Events

TechNexus Community runs a continuous calendar of technology-focused in-person and virtual events for learning, networking, innovation, and collaboration. Events are hosted in Bengaluru, Chennai, Srivilliputhur, partner campuses, and online/hybrid platforms. Many flagship events are hosted at Microsoft venues (Microsoft Ferns Bengaluru, Microsoft Reactor Bengaluru, Microsoft Office Chennai), as well as Yuniq Tidel BioPark Chennai, KARE Srivilliputhur, and Polaris School of Technology Bengaluru.

## Registration & Entry

Most events have controlled access: RSVP/registration, event pass or confirmation, joining the official TechNexus WhatsApp group, and showing confirmation at the venue. High-demand or Microsoft-hosted events require WhatsApp confirmation, double verification, and only approved participants with passes are admitted. Many events require arriving 30 minutes early for check-in.

## Communication

The TechNexus WhatsApp group is the primary channel for confirmations, approvals, entry instructions, agenda updates, and last-minute announcements.
