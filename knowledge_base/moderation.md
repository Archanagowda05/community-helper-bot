# TechNexus Moderation

## Moderation Authority

Moderators maintain safety, discipline, and professionalism across WhatsApp groups, online events, in-person events, and community discussions — monitoring activity, reviewing content, enforcing rules, and escalating serious violations to organizers.

## Moderator Powers

Delete inappropriate messages, mute users temporarily, remove members from groups, ban repeat offenders, and restrict event participation. Actions may be taken with or without warning depending on severity.

## Zero-Tolerance Violations

Immediate removal or ban for harassment, hate speech, gender discrimination, fraudulent activity, fake voucher distribution, impersonation, threats, disruptive behavior, plagiarism, bullying, or using the community for personal aspirations.

## Event Security

Physical event security is managed jointly by TechNexus organizers, volunteers, and venue security teams. Entry control includes pass verification, QR scanning, RSVP confirmation, and double WhatsApp verification for limited-seat events.

## Complaints

Complaints about misconduct, harassment, group disputes, voucher issues, or event access conflicts are handled by the moderation team and escalated to organizers when needed. Resolutions are reviewed internally and final once communicated.

## Enforcement Philosophy

Prevention → Warning → Removal → Ban. Priorities: community safety, inclusivity, professional learning environment, and brand integrity.
