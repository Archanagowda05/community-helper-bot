# Moderation

## Our Approach

Our moderation team is available **24/7** to ensure a safe and welcoming environment. We believe in fair, transparent, and consistent enforcement of community guidelines.

## How to Report Issues

If you encounter any issues:
- Use the `/report` command in Discord
- Contact a moderator directly via DM
- Email reports@community.example.com

All reports are handled **confidentially**. We never reveal who submitted a report.

## Strike System

Violations of community guidelines result in a **three-strike system**:

1. **First offense**: Written warning with explanation of the violation
2. **Second offense**: Temporary mute (24–72 hours depending on severity)
3. **Third offense**: Permanent ban from the community

**Severe violations** (hate speech, threats, doxxing, illegal activity) may result in **immediate permanent ban** without prior warnings.

## Appeals

Members who receive a ban may appeal by emailing appeals@community.example.com within 30 days. Appeals are reviewed by at least two admins who were not involved in the original decision.

## Moderator Guidelines

Moderators follow a documented internal policy to ensure consistency. They must:
- Remain impartial
- Document all actions taken
- Consult with other moderators on edge cases
- Never use moderation powers in personal disputes
