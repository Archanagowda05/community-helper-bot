# Community Roles

## Member (Default)

All new members start with this role. Members can:
- Participate in discussions
- Attend events
- Access public channels
- Share content and resources

## Contributor

Active participants who help others. Requirements:
- Be an active member for at least **3 months**
- Help answer questions regularly
- Be nominated by an existing Contributor or Moderator

**Benefits:**
- Access to exclusive channels
- Early event registration
- Contributor badge on profile
- Voting rights on community proposals

## Moderator

Community guardians who enforce guidelines. Moderators are selected from experienced Contributors who demonstrate:
- Strong understanding of community values
- Consistent positive engagement
- Ability to handle conflicts diplomatically

**Responsibilities:**
- Enforce community rules
- Handle reports and disputes
- Organize events
- Mentor new members

## Admin

The community leadership team responsible for:
- Strategic direction of the community
- Platform management
- Partnerships and sponsorships
- Final decisions on policy matters

## How Roles Are Earned

Roles are earned through consistent positive participation, not requested. Focus on being helpful, respectful, and engaged — recognition follows naturally.
