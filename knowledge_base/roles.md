# TechNexus Roles

## Community Members

Members participate in discussions, attend events, and collaborate on projects.

## Moderators

Moderators ensure that community rules are followed and help maintain a positive and inclusive environment.

## Organizers

Organizers are responsible for planning events, managing schedules, and coordinating community activities.

## Mentors

Mentors guide members by providing technical advice, project feedback, and career suggestions.
